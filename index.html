About This Platform

Welcome to the Flood Information Platform of Armenia. This citizen-science initiative allows users to visualize historical flood data verified by geolocation and video evidence.

How it Works

  • View: Browse the map to see flood events.
  • Filter: Use the time slider below to filter by date.
  • Contribute: Have a video of a flood? Click "Submit Video" to share it.
